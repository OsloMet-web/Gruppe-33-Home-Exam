Welcome to OsloMet Campus

OsloMet - Oslo Metropolitan University, located in the heart of Oslo, Norway, is a vibrant hub of academic activity and student life.

Known for its dynamic campus environment, OsloMet offers a wide range of study programs and research opportunities, making it one of the leading universities in the region.

Explore Campus

Pilestredet 35

Pilestredet 35, a notable building on the OsloMet campus, is a hub for student services and administrative offices. This building also houses several academic departments, offering students a centralized location for many of their needs.

Student Life and Facilities

The campus is bustling with student activities, clubs, and organizations, making it an exciting place for personal growth and social engagement. Facilities such as modern libraries, study areas, and computer labs support students in their academic pursuits.

Green and Sustainable Campus

OsloMet is committed to sustainability and has implemented various green initiatives. These include energy-efficient buildings, waste reduction programs, and encouraging sustainable transportation methods for students and staff.
